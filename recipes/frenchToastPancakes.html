French Toast Pancakes

French toast pancakes are a family favorite as we can never decide on French toast or pancakes! Great for any holiday breakfasts to keep everyone happy.

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons vegetable oil
  • 1 cup flour
  • White sugar
  • baking powder
  • salt
  • nutmeg
  • ground cinnamon
  • milk
  • egg
  • vanilla

Directions

  1. Heat oil over medium heat.
  2. Whisk
  3. Drop batter on skillet.
  4. Wait till bubbles form and flip.
  5. Cook for 3 to 4 min.
